Before Your First Class

Classes are for all ability levels, including students training for the first time. Kids should be at least ten years old, and the first two lessons are free for beginners and students new to the dojo.

Classes meet Mondays and Fridays from 6:00pm to 7:30pm, with the dojo opening at 5:30pm.
