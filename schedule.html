Class Schedule

Enshin Karate Schedule

Classes meet twice a week on Mondays and Fridays from 6:00pm to 7:30pm. The first two lessons are free for beginners and students new to the dojo.

Classes

Days Mondays and Fridays
Time 6:00pm-7:30pm. The dojo opens at 5:30pm and class starts promptly at 6:00pm.
Levels All ability levels, whether it is your first time, you have trained in another martial art, or you are currently a student at the dojo.
Ages All classes are mixed kids and adults. Kids should be at least ten years old.
Trial If you are interested, we are happy to have you try out a class.

Class Tuition

You may either pay for each individual class you attend or you may pay once to come to all of the classes that are offered in a given month.

$120 Monthly fee
$35 Per class
$60-80 Uniform / Light Weight Gi for kids
$110 Uniform / Heavy Weight Gi for adults

If you have a family of three or more students you will receive a $60 discount on monthly tuition.
